Boba History!

Boba is awesome, but have you ever truly known the true history of it?

Origin

1. Boba, or also known as bubble tea, originated in Taiwan in the 1980s!

2. The oldest known bubble tea drink consisted of a mixture of hot Taiwanese black tea, tapioca pearls" - Wikipedia

3. The name "boba" comes from the Chinese word "bōbō", which refers to the sound of bubbles popping in the tea" - Wikipedia
